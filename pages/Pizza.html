Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, olives, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Stir until the yeast is dissolved and let it sit for 5 minutes or until it starts to foam.
  2. Add 1 1/2 cups of the flour, olive oil, and salt to the bowl and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, adding more flour as needed.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 30 minutes or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to form a 12-inch (30 cm) circle.
  7. Place the dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  8. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch (1 cm) border.
  9. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your toppings.
  10. Bake the pizza in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  11. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
